✨ How To Play ✨
Welcome to the Kawaii Claw Machine! (◕‿◕✿)
🕹️
Controls
Use ← → ↑ ↓ Arrow Keys to move the claw around the machine.
Press Space or Enter to drop the claw and grab!
🪙
Tokens
You start with 5 tokens. Each grab costs 1 token.
When you run out, the game ends — but leftover tokens give you a huge cubic bonus (tokens³ × score), so grab wisely!
⭐
Scoring & Tickets
Each plushie is worth 14–25 points. You also earn tickets (1 ticket per 5 points).
Points and tickets stack up — aim for the highest score!
🔥
Bonuses
Multi-Catch: Grab 2+ plushies at once for quadratic bonus points + a bonus token!
✨ Shiny: One random plushie each game glows — catch it for ×10 points!
🎊 Perfect Game: Catch ALL plushies for a massive score² bonus!
📊
Stats & High Scores
Click the 📊 STATS button to see your lifetime stats, best scores, and full game history.
After each game you can 📸 export a report card PNG to share!
Good luck & have fun~! ♡(◕ᗜ◕✿)
